Crafting Your Dream Outdoor Space

At Oxford Builders, we understand that a deck is more than just an addition to your home; it's a space for relaxation, entertainment, and making lasting memories. Our team works closely with you to bring your vision to life, offering a range of decking solutions tailored to your specific needs and the unique characteristics of your Castle Hill property.

Our Decking Services Include:

  • Custom Deck Design: We create bespoke designs that perfectly complement your home's architecture and your lifestyle, ensuring functionality and aesthetic appeal.
  • Hardwood Decking: Utilizing premium local native species such as Merbau, Ironbark, Blackbutt, Spotted Gum, and Red Gum, we build durable and beautiful hardwood decks that stand the test of time.
  • Composite Decking: For those seeking low-maintenance and long-lasting options, our composite decks, made from a blend of plastic and wood fibres, offer superior longevity and resistance to weathering.
  • Treated Softwood Decking: Affordable and versatile options like pine or cedar are available for a cost-effective yet charming outdoor space.
  • Deck Restoration & Maintenance: Beyond new installations, we provide services to restore, stain, oil, and seal your existing deck, ensuring its beauty and longevity.
  • Decking Posts & Rails: We incorporate safety and style with custom posts and rails, completing the look and functionality of your deck.
